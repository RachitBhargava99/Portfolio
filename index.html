Empape

Empape is a job management application allowing you to stay ahead of the competition by giving you notifications ahead of time to go for jobs in the companies you are passionate about.

Empape uses web scraping to import data of jobs posted by companies in real time and allows you to see exactly what you might be interested in on one page without having you to move from one website to another except when you want to go to the company's website to apply for the job.

My Involvement in this Project: Full-Stack

Full Stack: Python, Flask, Google App Engine, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Greenhouse API

Spindler

Spindler is an image search engine that extracts all the images from NASA and SpaceX's public image archives and presents them to you based on the search queries you provide.

You have the ability to filter out the results based on the location where they were taken, name of the photographers who took the image, dates when the images were taken, where they were uploaded from, and many more super cool options like these.

Also, if you tend to keep track of the images you like or the images you searched for, the application allows you to add images to your personal favorites and also check your search history. If you are passionate about any topic, you may add the search criteria to your stream and any new images that meets the search criteria will be sent to you via email notifications.

My Involvement in this Project: Full-Stack

Full Stack: Python, Flask, Google App Engine, Google Compute Engine, Google Cloud SQL, MySQL, NASA's Image Archives API, SpaceX API, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

RoadAware

RoadAware is a road safety application that helps you avoid roads which have historically had a higher concentration of accidents.

As a city car driver, the application tracks your real-time location using your device's location services and shoots over a notification if you are approaching an area which has had a lot of accidents in near past.

As someone from the city administration, the application gives you an overview of the entire city summarizing which areas might be more prone to having accidents so that you can make better decisions as to where you might want to install accident prevention equipments like roundabouts or traffic signals.

My Involvement in this Project: Full-Stack

Full Stack: Python, Flask, Google App Engine, Google Compute Engine, Google Cloud SQL, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Google Maps JavaScript API, Google Maps Visualization API's

Event Management Protocol

Imagine yourself being asked by your boss to organize an exciting event. Chances are you are allocated a budget by your company and you are excited to take up the challenge. But wait, did you know managing events and its finances simultaneously can sometimes be a nightmare even for professional event managers?

Well, Event Management Protocol (EMP) comes to your rescue here. All you need to do is tell the system about the details of the event, including your budget. Once done, it broadcasts the information about your event throughout your company and invites everyone to join in. The application records the dietary preferences of the users and also takes care of how many people attend it.

My Involvement in this Project: Backend, Database Management

Full Stack: React, Redux, React Router, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Python, Flask, MySQL, Google Cloud SQL, Google App Engine, Google Maps Place Search API, Eventbrite API, Eventbrite Webhooks, Java, Android, XML
